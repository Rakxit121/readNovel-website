Our Advantages

1

Easy User interface and reading stories without pop-ups,hence there is no distraction.

There is no popup for inviting friends and there are no pop-ups for reading stories. The user interface does not have any distractions and the users can focus more on what they are looking at.

ReadNovel is a social book trading platform, where every reader can have equal opportunities to discover and read the best novels from different genres.

Original novels that everyone can read

ReadNovel is a new site for you to read light novels online. As their name suggests, they specialize in online light novel reading and even allow users who want to receive the next chapters of those novels that are on hiatus or has ended.
